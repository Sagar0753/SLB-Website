Embedded Systems Project
Traveler Pro X1
Smart Bag
An autonomous luggage system driven by embedded C logic. Uses RSSI-based signal tracking, IMU sensors, and GPS to follow its owner — hands-free.
Core Features
Auto-Follow System
BLE RSSI directional follow — bag sweeps and locks onto your signal continuously, adjusting motor PWM in real time.
GPS Tracking
Live coordinates from NMEA parsing on embedded module. Shows distance, speed, and heading.
Remote Control
Full manual D-pad control with speed adjustment over BLE or WiFi from any browser.
Anti-Theft Alert
Buzzer + vibration alarm when the bag is moved without authorization or signal is lost.
Signal Tracking
RSSI bar visualization with adaptive motor control based on proximity deltas from scanned positions.

Embedded System

How It Works
Signal Tracking
RSSI values sampled across directional sweeps. Bag rotates to maximize signal strength, homing onto the owner.
Compass & IMU
MPU-6050 provides yaw (heading) and acceleration data for movement detection and impact alerts.
Follow Logic
C control loop adjusts L/R motor PWM based on RSSI delta to continuously home in on the owner's device.
